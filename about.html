Raffaele Custom Woodworking

Originally from San Vito, Italy

Raffaele Gironda's passion for woodworking began when he was only 5 years old. He began assisting a local woodworker in his hometown, and he has been involved in woodworking ever since.

Before he began woodworking fulltime, Raffaele was an accomplished chef and entrepreneur. He worked at some of the finest restaurants in the country including locations such as New York, Kansas City, and South Florida.

He and his wife Lucy realized their dream of owning their own business when they moved to Pinehurst, North Carolina in 1980 and opened a restaurant of their own.

Raffaele's Restaurant served the Sandhills area of North Carolina for over 30 years, offering homemade Italian classics such as Lasagna and Chicken Parmesan, as well as one of a kind specials created by Chef Raffaele such as Rib-Eye Pepperonata and Donna's Chicken.

While still busy running the restaurant, Raffaele never stopped woodworking. Up early every morning, he would head to his woodshop at 6am and work there until it was time to open the restaurant in the afternoon. His work was always proudly on display at the restaurant and he constantly custom-made a wide variety of woodworking creations for his customers and friends.

Raffaele left the restaurant business in 2013 to pursue woodworking fulltime and he's been doing it ever since. He continues to craft one of a kind woodworking pieces every day in his shop.
